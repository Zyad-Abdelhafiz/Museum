King Tutankhamun

king Tutankhamun was an ancient Egyptian pharaoh who ruled from 1333 to 1323 BC. He was the ninth pharaoh of the Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt. Tutankhamun was a child when he became pharaoh, and he died at the age of 18 or 19.

SPHINX OF THUTMOSE III

The Sphinx of Thutmose III is a large limestone statue of the pharaoh Thutmose III, located in the Karnak Temple Complex in Luxor, Egypt. The sphinx is 7.25 meters (24 feet) long and 2.5 meters (8 feet) high.

TEMPLE OF NEFERTITI

THE TEMPLE OF NEFERTARI IS A SMALL TEMPLE LOCATED IN THE VALLEY OF THE QUEENS, NEAR LUXOR, EGYPT. IT WAS BUILT BY THE PHARAOH RAMESSES II FOR HIS WIFE, NEFERTARI. THE TEMPLE IS DEDICATED TO NEFERTARI AND THE GODDESS HATHOR.
